Support for Additional Schema Formats

Recognizing the diverse landscape of schema languages, the "XSD Visualizer Plugin" aims to broaden its support beyond XML Schema. The development team is actively working on incorporating support for other popular schema formats such as Schematron and Relax NG. Even JSON Schema is on the roadmap. This expansion will enable users to visualize and analyze schemas written in these languages, enhancing the overall versatility and usefulness of the plugin.
