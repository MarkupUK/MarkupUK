Markup UK Privacy Policy

Markup UK Conferences Ltd is committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about what information we may hold about you.  We take great care to comply with the requirements of the UK Data Protection Act 1998, The Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulation 2003 and the General Data Protection Regulation 2018 (GDPR) all relating to personal information you supply to us.

This privacy policy explains how we collect information from you when you use our website.

Information we may collect

Personal data

This includes information that you provide by registering on our website to attend our annual conference and may include the following:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Address
  • Dietary preferences

How will we use your data?

We only use your data to manage attendance at our annual conference.  We will only share your information to the extent required to manage the conference, for example with the venue provider in order to manage access to the building and catering requirements.

Our online payments are managed by Paypal.

We will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy.

Marketing

We do not share your data with partners without your express permission.  We may ask if you would be happy for us to provide your name and email address to our sister conferences (as listed on our website).

We may send you updates and follow ups about the conference you have attended or expressed an interest in.  If you do not wish to be contacted, please email info@markupuk.org

What are your data protection rights?

Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access

You have the right to request copies of your personal data.  We may charge you a small fee for this service.

The right to rectification

You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.  You also have the right to request that we complete any information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure

You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing

You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing

You have the right to object to the processing of your data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability

You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organisation or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you.  If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please email us at info@markupuk.org

We do not use cookies.
