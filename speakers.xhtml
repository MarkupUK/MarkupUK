Abstract Submissions now open!

Do you have something that you could share with a larger audience? Now would be a good time to send us an extended abstract or even a full paper.

We are excited to hear your ideas, however they relate to markup languages. We are even more excited if the ideas fit one or more of the following conference themes…

  • Markup in digital humanities, publishing, or education

    • User Stories

    • Challenges

    • Topic based publishing

    • Single Source publishing

  • Managing metadata in publishing

  • Trees and Graphs: conflicts, overlap, and sweet spots

The conference audience will be a good mixture of markup geeks, digital humanists, end users, and an occasional decision maker.

When you submit an abstract, it will be reviewed by our “Programme Committee”. Once your abstract would be accepted, we expect you to commit to writing a full paper for the proceedings, and a 25 minute presentation during the conference.

Abstracts can be submitted using the Microsoft conference management toolkit, located at https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/MARKUPUK2018. Some people have reported issues when submitting their abstracts. Please let us know if you experience problems when submitting, at info@markupuk.org

We would like to encourage first-time speakers. If you are in doubt whether your ideas would match the audience expectations, or if you would prefer a slot that is somewhat smaller than the envisaged 25 minutes, please do not hesitate to contact us to discuss your ideas at info@markupuk.org.

Speaker Registration

Accepted speakers get a full conference pass that includes the social dinner. If you have submitted a paper, please don't register before the speakers have been notified.
