Call for Papers Now Open!

The theme for Markup UK 2019 will be "Beyond the Bricks".

We encourage stories about bringing markup building blocks together to form packaged solutions or frameworks.

We would love to hear about endeavours to shorten the distance between using the bricks and building the house: orchestration, continuous delivery, testing solutions, managment, DocOps solutions...

Of course, we welcome quality contributions on any markup-related theme, particularly those about the use of markup in the humanities, linguistics, cultural heritage, publishing, and education:

  • User stories
  • Challenges
  • Topic-based publishing
  • Single-source publishing

At the moment we only need extended abstracts (500 words or more) - full papers will be required later!

We will use the microsoft CMT system again this year for managing the abstract submissions and peer review. Before submitting an abstract, please register as an author at https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/MARKUPUK2019/Submission/Index.

Paper Submission

Before submitting an abstract, you need to register as an author at https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/MARKUPUK2019/Submission/Index.

The conference audience will be a good mixture of markup geeks, digital humanists, end users, and an occasional decision maker.

When you submit an abstract, it will be reviewed by our “Programme Committee”. If your abstract is accepted, we expect you to commit to writing a full paper for the proceedings, and a 25 minute presentation during the conference.

We would like to encourage first-time speakers. If you are in doubt whether your ideas would match the audience expectations, or if you would prefer a slot that is somewhat smaller than the envisaged 25 minutes, please do not hesitate to contact us to discuss your ideas at info@markupuk.org.
