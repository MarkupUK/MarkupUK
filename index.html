Conference theme 2019: "Beyond the Bricks"

Markup UK is a conference for anyone interested in challenges, solutions, and recent developments in the use of XML and other markup technologies.

After decades of development, improvement, and diversification, mature markup technologies now offer a diverse range of building blocks. These markup "bricks" are combined to construct elegant frameworks and packaged solutions, to streamline production processes, to build creative applications and scholarly projects, and much more.

At Markup UK 2019, join a broad group of technologists, end-users, publishers, and academics to explore approaches to constructing with markup technologies.
