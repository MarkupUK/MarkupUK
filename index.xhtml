XProc Next Workshop

The XProc Next W3C Community Group will hold a two-day workshop right after Markup UK, on June 11–12, and anyone with an interest in XProc and XML pipelining is encouraged to participate. MarkLogic has graciously offered to host the workshop in in their London offices at Bank.
